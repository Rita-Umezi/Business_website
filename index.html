Crafting Healthier Habits in Every Bite

Ray’s Kitchen was born out of a simple realization: finding food that is both convenient and genuinely healthy shouldn't be a struggle. We started in Asaba with a mission to redefine the "snack" by packing every jar and box with nutrients that fuel your body.

From our signature cashew-topped banana bread to our cold-pressed ginger pineapple blends, every ingredient is chosen for its benefit. We believe in transparency—no hidden sugars, no artificial fillers, just real food made by real people who care about your wellness.
